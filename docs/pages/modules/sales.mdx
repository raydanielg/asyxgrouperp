# Sales & Invoicing

Complete sales management from quotation to payment collection.

## Sales Dashboard

A unified view of all sales activities.

**Displays:**
- Today's sales summary
- Monthly trends
- Top customers
- Outstanding receivables
- Recent proposals and invoices
- Conversion rates

**Navigation:** Admin → Sales → Sales Dashboard

## Quotations (Sales Proposals)

Create professional quotations for potential clients.

**Features:**
- Line items with quantity, rate, tax
- Company branding on PDF
- Status: Draft → Sent → Accepted → Rejected → Expired
- Convert accepted proposals to:
  - Sales Invoice
  - Project
- Validity period
- Terms and conditions

**Navigation:** Admin → Sales → Quotations

## Sales Invoices

Full invoicing with payment tracking.

**Features:**
- Auto-generated invoice numbers
- Line items with description, quantity, rate
- Tax/VAT calculation (18%)
- Discount support
- Payment recording (partial & full)
- Status workflow: Draft → Posted → Partial → Paid / Overdue
- PDF generation with company letterhead
- Email to customer
- Due date tracking

**Navigation:** Admin → Sales → Sales Invoices

## Sales Returns

Process customer returns and issue credit notes.

**Features:**
- Return against specific invoice
- Quantity and reason tracking
- Automatic balance adjustment
- Return approval workflow
- Stock adjustment trigger

**Navigation:** Admin → Sales → Sales Returns

## Tax Invoices

Generate tax-compliant invoices from completed projects.

**Features:**
- Auto-populated from project data
- VAT calculation at 18%
- 30-day payment terms (configurable)
- Tax registration number display
- Linked to project and original proposal

## Payment Recording

Record payments against invoices:
1. Open the invoice
2. Click "Record Payment"
3. Enter amount, date, payment method
4. System updates balance and status automatically
