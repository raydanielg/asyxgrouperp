# Inventory & Warehouse Management

Manage products, stock levels, warehouses, and inter-warehouse transfers.

## Products

Maintain your product catalog with stock tracking.

**Features:**
- Product creation with SKU, description, pricing
- Category classification
- Stock quantity tracking per warehouse
- Reorder level alerts
- Product images
- Cost price and selling price
- Barcode support

**Navigation:** Admin → Products & Inventory → Products

## Product Categories

Organize products into hierarchical categories.

**Features:**
- Category tree structure
- Bulk categorization
- Category-based reporting

**Navigation:** Admin → Products & Inventory → Categories

## Suppliers

Manage your vendor/supplier database.

**Features:**
- Supplier profiles with contact details
- Product linkage
- Purchase history
- Payment terms
- Rating/feedback

**Navigation:** Admin → Products & Inventory → Suppliers

## Stock Movements

Track all inventory movements.

**Features:**
- Inbound movements (purchases, returns)
- Outbound movements (sales, damages)
- Adjustment entries
- Movement history with timestamps
- User tracking (who made the movement)

**Navigation:** Admin → Products & Inventory → Stock Movements

## Warehouses

Manage multiple storage locations.

**Features:**
- Warehouse creation with address
- Capacity tracking
- Active/inactive status
- Stock levels per warehouse
- Warehouse manager assignment

**Navigation:** Admin → Inventory → Warehouses

## Inter-Warehouse Transfers

Move stock between warehouses.

**Features:**
- Transfer request creation
- Source and destination warehouse
- Item quantity specification
- Status tracking: Pending → In Transit → Received
- Transfer notes
- Approval workflow

**Navigation:** Admin → Inventory → Transfers

## Low Stock Alerts

The system automatically identifies products below reorder levels:
- Dashboard displays low-stock count
- Products list highlights items needing reorder
- Notifications sent to logistics officers
