# Business Flow

The Business Flow module manages the complete procurement-to-delivery cycle.

## Flow Overview

```
Tender → Quotation → Budget → LPO → GRN → Delivery Note → Vendor Invoice → Payment
```

## Tenders

Manage tender/bid processes.

**Features:**
- Tender creation with specifications
- Submission deadline
- Vendor invitations
- Bid evaluation
- Award decision
- Status tracking

**Navigation:** Admin → Business Flow → Tenders

## Quotations (Procurement)

Request and evaluate vendor quotations.

**Features:**
- RFQ (Request for Quotation) creation
- Multi-vendor comparison
- Line item pricing
- Validity period
- Selection and award

**Navigation:** Admin → Business Flow → Quotations

## Project Budgets

Budget planning and tracking per project.

**Features:**
- Budget allocation by category
- Actual vs planned comparison
- Budget approval workflow
- Variance reporting
- Multi-currency support

**Navigation:** Admin → Business Flow → Project Budgets

## Local Purchase Orders (LPOs)

Formal purchase orders to vendors.

**Features:**
- LPO creation with items
- Auto-numbering
- Vendor selection
- Terms and conditions
- Approval workflow
- PDF generation
- Status tracking (draft, approved, sent, fulfilled)

**Navigation:** Admin → Business Flow → LPOs

## Goods Received Notes (GRNs)

Record receipt of ordered goods.

**Features:**
- Link to LPO
- Quantity verification
- Quality inspection notes
- Partial receipt support
- Discrepancy reporting
- Stock update trigger

**Navigation:** Admin → Business Flow → GRNs

## Delivery Notes

Track outbound deliveries to customers.

**Features:**
- Delivery note generation
- Link to sales invoice
- Item listing with quantities
- Driver/vehicle assignment
- Customer signature capture
- Status tracking

**Navigation:** Admin → Business Flow → Delivery Notes

## Vendor Invoices

Record and process vendor invoices.

**Features:**
- Invoice entry from vendor
- Match against GRN/LPO
- 3-way matching (LPO, GRN, Invoice)
- Payment scheduling
- Dispute management

**Navigation:** Admin → Business Flow → Vendor Invoices

## Office Expenses

Track daily office operational expenses.

**Navigation:** Admin → Business Flow → Office Expenses

## Client Receipts

Record payments received from clients.

**Navigation:** Admin → Business Flow → Client Receipts
