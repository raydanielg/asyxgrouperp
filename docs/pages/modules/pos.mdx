# Point of Sale (POS)

A full-featured POS terminal for retail and counter sales.

## POS Terminal

Quick sales entry with product lookup and payment processing.

**Features:**
- Product search by name, SKU, or barcode
- Quick-add buttons for popular items
- Quantity adjustment
- Discount application (per item or total)
- Multiple payment methods (cash, card, mobile money)
- Receipt generation
- Customer assignment (optional)
- Hold and recall transactions

**Navigation:** Admin → POS → POS Terminal

## How to Make a Sale

1. Open POS Terminal
2. Search or scan products
3. Adjust quantities as needed
4. Apply discounts if applicable
5. Select payment method
6. Process payment
7. Print or email receipt

## POS Reports

Comprehensive sales analytics.

**Reports Include:**
- Daily sales summary
- Monthly sales trends
- Product-wise sales breakdown
- Payment method distribution
- Hourly sales patterns
- Cashier performance
- Top-selling products

**Navigation:** Admin → POS → POS Reports

## Cash Management

- Opening balance recording
- Cash drawer reconciliation
- End-of-day closure
- Over/short tracking

## Integration

POS sales automatically:
- Reduce product stock levels
- Create entries in sales records
- Update daily/monthly dashboards
- Reflect in financial reports
