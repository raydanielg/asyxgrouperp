# Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

The CRM module helps you manage customer relationships from initial lead to closed deal and ongoing contract management.

## Leads

Track potential customers through your sales pipeline.

**Features:**
- Lead capture with source tracking (website, referral, cold call, etc.)
- Lead scoring and qualification
- Assignment to sales agents
- Status progression: New → Contacted → Qualified → Converted/Lost
- Follow-up scheduling
- Conversion to deal/contact

**Navigation:** Admin → CRM → Leads

**Lead Statuses:**
| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| New | Just captured, not yet contacted |
| Contacted | Initial outreach made |
| Qualified | Confirmed as a viable opportunity |
| Converted | Successfully converted to deal |
| Lost | Did not convert |

## Deals

Manage sales opportunities and track deal progress.

**Features:**
- Deal pipeline management
- Deal value and expected close date
- Stage progression tracking
- Win/loss tracking with reasons
- Deal notes and activity log

**Navigation:** Admin → CRM → Deals

## Contracts

Manage customer contracts and service agreements.

**Features:**
- Contract creation with terms
- Start/end date tracking
- Auto-renewal alerts
- Contract value tracking
- Document attachments
- Status management (draft, active, expired, terminated)

**Navigation:** Admin → CRM → Contracts

## Contacts

Maintain a centralized contact database.

**Features:**
- Contact profiles with complete details
- Company association
- Communication history
- Tag/category classification
- Import/export functionality

**Navigation:** Admin → CRM → Contacts

## Call Center Integration

The Call Center module extends CRM with telephony features.

**Features:**
- Call logging
- Lead assignment to agents
- Follow-up scheduling
- Performance metrics per agent
- Quick actions for lead management

**Navigation:** Admin → Call Center → Dashboard
