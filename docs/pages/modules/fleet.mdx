# Fleet Management

Track and manage company vehicles, maintenance schedules, and assignments.

## Vehicle Registry

Maintain a database of all company vehicles.

**Features:**
- Vehicle details (make, model, year, color)
- Registration number (unique identifier)
- Insurance information and expiry
- Purchase date and value
- Current mileage
- Status: Active → Under Maintenance → Retired
- Company assignment (multi-company)

**Navigation:** Admin → Fleet & Assets → Vehicles

## Vehicle Assignment

Assign vehicles to employees or departments.

**Features:**
- Employee-vehicle mapping
- Assignment history
- Department allocation
- Usage tracking

## Maintenance Tracking

Schedule and record vehicle maintenance.

**Features:**
- Scheduled maintenance alerts
- Service history logging
- Cost tracking per vehicle
- Vendor/garage management
- Parts replacement records

## Fuel Management

Track fuel consumption and costs.

**Features:**
- Fuel entry logging
- Cost per kilometer calculation
- Monthly fuel reports
- Anomaly detection

## Fixed Assets

Beyond vehicles, manage all company fixed assets.

**Features:**
- Asset registration with details
- Depreciation calculation
- Location tracking
- Assignment to employees
- Disposal management
- Asset categories

**Navigation:** Admin → Fleet & Assets → Fixed Assets
