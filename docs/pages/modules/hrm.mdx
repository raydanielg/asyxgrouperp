# Human Resource Management (HRM)

The HRM module is the backbone of people management in ASYX Group ERP. It handles everything from employee onboarding to payroll processing.

## Modules Overview

### Employees
Manage your entire workforce with detailed profiles.

**Features:**
- Employee directory with search and filters
- Personal information, emergency contacts, bank details
- Department and designation assignment
- Employment status tracking (active, probation, terminated)
- Document attachments (ID, contracts, certificates)
- Salary and compensation management

**Navigation:** Admin → HRM → Employees

### Attendance

Track employee attendance with clock-in/clock-out functionality.

**Features:**
- Daily attendance marking (present, absent, late, half-day)
- Clock-in/clock-out with timestamps
- Overtime tracking
- Monthly attendance reports
- Attendance correction requests

**Navigation:** Admin → HRM → Attendance

### Payroll

Generate and manage monthly payroll with statutory deductions.

**Features:**
- Automatic payroll generation from employee salaries
- Statutory deductions:
  - **NSSF** (National Social Security Fund) — 10% employee + 10% employer
  - **NHIF** (National Health Insurance Fund) — tiered rates
  - **PAYE** (Pay As You Earn) — progressive tax brackets
- Loan/advance deductions
- Payslip PDF generation
- Payroll history and reports
- Company-context aware generation

**Navigation:** Admin → HRM → Payroll

**Generating Payroll:**
1. Navigate to Payroll page
2. Select month and year
3. Click "Generate Payroll"
4. System calculates gross pay, deductions, and net pay
5. Review and approve

### Leaves

Manage employee leave requests and approvals.

**Features:**
- Leave request submission
- Multiple leave types (annual, sick, maternity, paternity, etc.)
- Leave balance tracking
- Approval workflow (pending → approved/rejected)
- Leave calendar view

**Navigation:** Admin → HRM → Leaves

### Performance Reviews

Conduct and track employee performance evaluations.

**Features:**
- Performance review cycles
- Rating scales and criteria
- Self-assessment and manager review
- Performance goals and objectives
- Historical performance data

**Navigation:** Admin → HRM → Performance

### Training

Manage employee training programs and certifications.

**Features:**
- Training program creation
- Employee enrollment
- Training status tracking
- Certificate management
- Training budget tracking

**Navigation:** Admin → HRM → Training

### Policies

Maintain company HR policies accessible to all employees.

**Features:**
- Policy document management
- Version control
- Department-specific policies
- Acknowledgment tracking

**Navigation:** Admin → HRM → Policies

### Events

Manage company events and celebrations.

**Features:**
- Event creation and scheduling
- Employee notifications
- Event categories
- RSVP tracking

**Navigation:** Admin → HRM → Events

## Payroll Deduction Calculations

### NSSF (Tanzania)
```
Employee contribution: 10% of gross salary
Employer contribution: 10% of gross salary
Maximum contributory earnings: TZS 720,000/month
```

### NHIF (Tanzania)
| Salary Range (TZS) | Monthly Contribution |
|---------------------|---------------------|
| Up to 150,000 | 5,100 |
| 150,001 - 250,000 | 8,500 |
| 250,001 - 350,000 | 11,900 |
| 350,001 - 450,000 | 15,300 |
| 450,001 - 600,000 | 20,400 |
| 600,001+ | 25,500 |

### PAYE (Tanzania)
| Monthly Income (TZS) | Rate |
|----------------------|------|
| 0 - 270,000 | 0% |
| 270,001 - 520,000 | 8% |
| 520,001 - 760,000 | 20% |
| 760,001 - 1,000,000 | 25% |
| Above 1,000,000 | 30% |
