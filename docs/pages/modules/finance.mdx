# Finance & Accounting

The Finance module provides comprehensive financial management including invoicing, expenses, revenues, and bank account management.

## Sales Invoices

Create and manage customer invoices with full lifecycle tracking.

**Features:**
- Invoice creation with line items
- Auto-numbering (INV-0001, INV-0002...)
- Status workflow: Draft → Posted → Partial → Paid / Overdue
- Payment recording
- PDF invoice generation
- VAT/Tax calculations (18% VAT)
- Customer management integration
- Due date tracking with overdue alerts

**Navigation:** Admin → Sales → Sales Invoices

## Purchase Invoices

Track vendor bills and supplier payments.

**Features:**
- Purchase invoice entry from vendors
- Payment status tracking
- Vendor management
- Balance tracking per vendor
- Purchase return processing

**Navigation:** Admin → Purchase → Purchase Invoices

## Expenses

Record and categorize business expenses.

**Features:**
- Expense entry with categories
- Receipt attachment (file uploads)
- Expense approval workflow
- Monthly/yearly expense reports
- Budget vs actual comparison

**Navigation:** Admin → Accounting → Expenses

## Revenue

Track all business income sources.

**Features:**
- Revenue recording by category
- Bank account linkage
- Monthly revenue trends
- Multi-company revenue tracking

**Navigation:** Admin → Accounting → Revenue

## Bank Accounts

Manage company bank accounts and balances.

**Features:**
- Multiple bank account support
- Account balance tracking
- Inter-account transfers
- Transaction history
- Bank reconciliation

**Navigation:** Admin → Accounting → Bank Accounts

## Estimates

Create quotes and estimates for potential clients.

**Features:**
- Estimate creation with line items
- Convert estimate to invoice
- Status tracking (draft, sent, accepted, rejected)
- PDF export

**Navigation:** Admin → Accounting → Estimates

## Bills

Manage recurring and one-time bills.

**Features:**
- Bill entry and tracking
- Payment scheduling
- Overdue bill alerts
- Bill categories

**Navigation:** Admin → Accounting → Bills

## Financial Reports

Generate comprehensive financial reports.

**Key Reports:**
- Profit & Loss Statement
- Revenue vs Expenses (monthly trend)
- Outstanding receivables
- Outstanding payables
- Cash flow summary
- Department-wise expense breakdown

**Navigation:** Admin → Reports

## Sales Returns & Purchase Returns

Handle product returns and credit notes.

**Sales Returns:**
- Create return against sales invoice
- Automatic balance adjustment
- Return reason tracking

**Purchase Returns:**
- Return goods to vendor
- Credit note generation
- Stock adjustment
